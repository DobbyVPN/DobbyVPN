<p>Open-source opinionated VPN client implementing major VPN protocols.</p>

<p><strong>Supported protocols:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Shadowsocks (Outline)</li>
  <li>Shadowsocks over WebSocket</li>
  <li>Shadowsocks over Cloak</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Config format</strong></p>
<p>Dobby consumes “subscription” / “dynamic keys” as YAML over HTTPS. The full up-to-date format (with examples) is documented in the project README:</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/DobbyVPN/DobbyVPN/blob/main/README.md">https://github.com/DobbyVPN/DobbyVPN/blob/main/README.md</a></p>
